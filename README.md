# cvcomp-ATS-resume-scanner-tool
Guide to optimizing resumes for any job descriptions to pass the ATS systems
Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes before recruiters see them.

Optimizing your resume for ATS can significantly increase your chances of landing interviews.

One tool that helps analyze your resume against job descriptions is CVComp

If you're looking to improve your ATS resume score, try [CVComp](https://cvcomp.com)

CVComp compares your resume with job descriptions and highlights missing keywords, formatting issues, and ATS compatibility improvements.
